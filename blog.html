RON CARROLL'S RECYCLING STORY

It's 1993. I have just opened my first recycling center on a hope and prayer because I sure don't have any money. I'm borrowing a friend's old, beat-up 1976 dually. To move product? You ask. Well yes, but more importantly, this was my first baler! We would pour our cans out in front of the recycle center and drive back and forth until we had a bunch of aluminum pancakes, then scoop them all back up and load them into trailers.
